  About our personalized service:

Essentially, a lighthouse is a tower or a building that emits lights with the use of lamps and lenses. These lights serve as a navigational aid for maritime pilots at sea or on inland waterways. Lighthouze décor is a service that offers small lighthouses built on the same principles and ideas.

Every human being goes through changes in life. These changes and the experiences that bring about these changes are what make us who we are. Another way to look at this would be to imagine a ship lost at sea. Wandering and taking each wave as a challenge, moving forward but with no direction or aim. The lighthouse, in this case, serves as a guiding light allowing the ship to find some direction and navigate the seas.

Our Lighthouze lamps are designed to add charisma to your home and along with it, suit your interior décor and desires to create the perfect lamp. One that will mesmerize you and add to your house. One that will light up that dark. Most of all, one that will guide your wandering soul, giving light to ideas and dreams in your head; making them a reality.

Lighthouze is more than just a lamp, it is a guide for your wandering ship!

 

  Our story:

Lighthouze was started in 2017 when our founder Vibha Madan thought of an amazing idea to light up one’s house. Being an interior designer allowed her to look into different ways of decorating one’s house. Looking at her own interiors for inspiration, she realized that lighting was a crucial part of décor.

She looked at many different ways of installing light fixtures until she came along the perfect idea: lights and potpourri held together in a small jar making a lamp. This jar resembled a lighthouse and the light functioned as a beacon for attention giving rise to the name ‘Lighthouze’.

Lighthouze is now a service run to serve the customers and create the most personalized and beautiful hand-crafted lamps for each and every household. These can be customized in terms of size, brightness and colour. Lighthouze is currently based in UAE and is looking to expand worldwide soon.

  Our quality assurance checks:

Each Lighthouze product is made with the customer in mind and goes through a range of quality checks to assure the perfect Lighthouze, each and every time. To assure this, our QA team checks the following things at every stage:

  • The Jars: Glass is a very fragile material and our team checks the quality of the glass for any cracks of scratches on the surface. Once it proves to be perfect, the jar moves on to the next stage of becoming a Lighthouze.
  • Frosting & Coating: The frosting or coating done on the glass has to be done with care. Once this is done, it is checked for any gaps and is tested to make sure it does not fade away with time. Once this proves to be perfect, the foundations for the Lighthouze are laid.
  • Potpourri: The potpourri is handpicked and checked. The colours are sorted in different sections to create different Lighthouzes for different styles, moods and even seasons!
  • Lights: Each and every bulb inside the Lighthouze is tested to make sure it works perfectly in the circuit.
  • Battery: The installed battery packs are tested for leaks and for full charge to last the longest.
  • Exterior Décor: After installation, the exterior of the glass jar is decorated to suit the design scheme.
  • The Lighthouze: The final product is checked, tested and packed with the utmost precision and care. It is then made ready for delivery.
